Welcome

This portal is dedicated to empowering women by providing information, resources, and complaint channels against workplace sexual harassment. It aims to create safer and more respectful workplaces across the state.

Our Goals

  • Ensure every woman knows her rights.
  • Provide quick access to legal support.
  • Spread awareness about workplace dignity.
