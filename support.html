Support & Complaint

We’re here to help you take action

Need Help?

If you are facing any form of workplace harassment, you can file a complaint through the following channels:

  • Contact the Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) at your workplace.
  • Call the National Women Helpline: 181
  • Email: support@womensafety.gov.in

Immediate Actions

Keep evidence, reach out to trusted colleagues, and contact legal aid services through our portal.
