About the Portal

Empowering women with awareness and justice

Our Mission

Our mission is to build a safe work environment where every woman is respected and protected from harassment. This platform serves as a digital bridge between victims and the state authorities.

Objectives

  • To inform women about their rights and available legal remedies.
  • To simplify the complaint registration process.
  • To offer access to helplines and legal advisors.
