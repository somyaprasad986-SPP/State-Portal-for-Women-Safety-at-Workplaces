Legal Rights & Acts

Know your rights, fight with confidence

Key Laws Protecting Women

  1. Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace Act (2013) – Ensures a safe workplace environment.
  2. Indian Penal Code Section 354A – Defines and punishes sexual harassment.
  3. POSH Rules – Guidelines for Internal Complaints Committees (ICCs).

Your Rights Include:

  • The right to file a complaint confidentially.
  • Protection from victimization.
  • Right to a safe working environment.
